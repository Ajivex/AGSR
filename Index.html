What We Do

Engineering Services

Comprehensive engineering capabilities delivered with precision, safety, and efficiency across Singapore's industrial landscape.

Mechanical Engineering

Design, fabrication, and installation of mechanical systems including rotating equipment, heat exchangers, pressure vessels, and process piping for industrial facilities.

Electrical Works

Licensed electrical contracting services covering LV/HV installation, switchboard upgrades, motor control centers, instrumentation, and industrial wiring.

Piping Fabrication

Custom piping design, fabrication, and installation for process plants. We handle carbon steel, stainless steel, duplex, and GRE piping systems to ASME standards.

Structural Fabrication

Steel structural fabrication and erection for platforms, skids, mezzanines, cable trays, and industrial support structures, designed and built to code.

Plant Maintenance

Scheduled and emergency plant maintenance services — turnaround support, predictive maintenance, mechanical overhauls, and shutdown planning for process plants.

Safety & Inspection

Statutory inspection, non-destructive testing (NDT), hydrostatic testing, and safety audits to ensure your plant operates within regulatory compliance at all times.
